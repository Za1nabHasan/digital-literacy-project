Social Media Do's and Don'ts Checklist

# Do's :
1. Use respectful and professional language in posts and comments.
2. Verify information before sharing news, updates, or screenshots.
3. Protect personal information such as phone number, address, and passwords.
4. Use strong passwords and enable two-factor authentication.
5. Maintain a positive digital presence that reflects your academic and professional goals.

# Don'ts :
1. Do not share confidential academic material or personal details publicly.
2. Do not post offensive, abusive, or inappropriate content.
3. Do not click suspicious links or respond to unknown messages.
4. Do not spread misinformation or unverified news.
5. Do not use social media during lectures or academic activities irresponsibly.

Stay Safe Online Checklist

1. Use strong and unique passwords for all important accounts, and enable two-factor authentication wherever possible. 
2. Never share OTPs, UPI PINs, passwords, or banking details with anyone, even if they claim to be from the bank. 
3. Verify links and website URLs carefully before clicking, especially in emails, SMS, or WhatsApp messages. 
4. Avoid using public Wi-Fi for online banking, UPI payments, or accessing sensitive accounts. 
5. Keep your phone, laptop, browser, and apps updated to the latest versions for security patches. 
6. For UPI safety: Never scan unknown QR codes to receive money, as QR codes are usually used to make payments, not receive them. 
7. For financial safety: Check the receiver’s UPI ID and name carefully before confirming any payment. 
8. Do not download APK files, unknown attachments, or apps from unofficial sources. 
9. Regularly review bank statements and UPI transaction history for suspicious activity. 
10. In case of cyber fraud or suspicious financial activity, immediately report it on the National Cyber Crime Portal: cybercrime.gov.in or call the Cyber Crime Helpline: 1930 for quick action. Quick reporting can help freeze fraudulent transactions.
